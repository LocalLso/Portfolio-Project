About


The school mathslab is a small project, which is intend to make learning easy!

This application is build in an effort to improve learning systems within the country that is centered on students and focusing on problem-solving methodology. This is also made with love in an endless effort to improve mathematics in the country, by having the same content being taught by different instructors and students being able to choose the instructor that they feel like explains best for them and having the option to learn from different instructors in the comfort of their homes.
This web application is intended to help instructors to further continue engaging with their students whether in camp or not. This will facilitate a smooth learning tremendously, indtuctors will habe the platform to issue learning material irrespective of their location - so long as they are to access the application, and be able to access students. This will be possible, because of the features of the application. Thus the instructors will be able to upload notes, tests, assignment, and solutions to students.
Students in turn, will be able to learn 24/7 due to the uptime of the application. Thus the world will be their oyster. Learning will be in their hands. They will be able to take tests online, read or download course material uploaded by their instructor. They can even participate in maths contest within the application as an effort to encourage learning by doing.
